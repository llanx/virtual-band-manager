# Song Structure Templates

## Template 1: Standard Pop/K-pop (3:20 - 3:40)

```
[Intro]              8-12 bars    ~15s    Instrumental or vocal hook teaser
[Verse 1]            8-16 bars    ~25s    Story setup, usually 1-2 members
[Pre-Chorus]         4-8 bars     ~15s    Tension build, shift energy upward
[Chorus]             8-16 bars    ~30s    Full group or lead + harmonies, hook
[Verse 2]            8-16 bars    ~25s    Story development, different members
[Pre-Chorus]         4-8 bars     ~15s    Variation on first pre-chorus
[Chorus]             8-16 bars    ~30s    Same hook, possibly bigger arrangement
[Bridge]             4-8 bars     ~15s    Emotional shift, stripped back or new feel
[Final Chorus]       8-16 bars    ~30s    Biggest version, key change optional
[Outro]              4-8 bars     ~10s    Fade or decisive ending
```

**Total: ~3:30**

**Member distribution guide:**
- Verse 1: Member A (usually a stronger vocalist)
- Verse 2: Member B (contrasting style)
- Pre-Chorus: Member C or shared
- Chorus: Lead vocal (Serena) + full group harmonies
- Bridge: Most emotionally open member or unexpected choice
- Ad-libs throughout: energetic member (Mika)

## Template 2: Dance/EDM-Influenced (3:20 - 3:50)

```
[Intro]              4-8 bars     ~10s    Beat intro or synth riff
[Verse 1]            8-16 bars    ~25s    Vocal verse over building beat
[Pre-Chorus]         4-8 bars     ~15s    Rising tension, filter sweeps
[Chorus]             8-16 bars    ~30s    Full drop, vocal hook
[Post-Chorus]        4-8 bars     ~10s    Instrumental hook or chant
[Verse 2]            8-16 bars    ~25s    Second verse, new member
[Pre-Chorus]         4-8 bars     ~15s    Build-up intensifies
[Chorus]             8-16 bars    ~30s    Bigger drop
[Dance Break]        8-16 bars    ~20s    Instrumental, beat switch or breakdown
[Final Chorus]       8-16 bars    ~30s    Biggest drop, all elements combined
[Outro]              4 bars       ~8s     Quick cut or fade
```

**Total: ~3:38**

**Member distribution guide:**
- Verses: Individual members
- Pre-Chorus: Shared or building layers (Member A line, Member B line, etc.)
- Chorus: Lead + group
- Post-Chorus: Chant section (all members) or instrumental spotlight
- Dance Break: Drums/bass spotlight (Mika + Evie), minimal vocals

## Template 3: Rap/Hip-Hop Hybrid (3:10 - 3:40)

```
[Intro]              4-8 bars     ~10s    Beat drop or spoken word
[Verse 1 - Rap]      16 bars      ~30s    Primary rapper/rhythmic vocalist
[Pre-Chorus]         4-8 bars     ~12s    Melodic transition
[Chorus]             8-16 bars    ~25s    Sung hook, catchy and repeatable
[Verse 2 - Rap]      16 bars      ~30s    Second rapper or same with new flow
[Pre-Chorus]         4-8 bars     ~12s    Variation
[Chorus]             8-16 bars    ~25s    Hook repeat
[Rap Break]          8-16 bars    ~20s    Double-time or freestyle section
[Bridge]             4-8 bars     ~12s    Stripped back, emotional vocal
[Final Chorus]       8-16 bars    ~25s    Hook with ad-libs layered
[Outro]              4 bars       ~8s     Beat outro
```

**Total: ~3:29**

**Member distribution guide:**
- Rap Verses: Mika (rhythmic delivery) or Kaia (attitude)
- Pre-Chorus: Serena or Evie (melodic bridge between rap and chorus)
- Chorus: Serena lead + group harmonies
- Rap Break: Whoever didn't get a verse, or double-time showcase
- Bridge: Most unexpected vocal (Evie's smooth contralto works well here)

## Template 4: Ballad / Emotional (3:40 - 4:30)

```
[Intro]              4-8 bars     ~15s    Piano, guitar, or strings -- sparse
[Verse 1]            8-16 bars    ~35s    Quiet, intimate, single voice
[Verse 2]            8-16 bars    ~35s    Second voice, building slightly
[Pre-Chorus]         4-8 bars     ~15s    Emotional build
[Chorus]             8-16 bars    ~35s    Full emotional release
[Verse 3]            8-16 bars    ~30s    Third perspective or story development
[Pre-Chorus]         4-8 bars     ~15s    More intense build
[Chorus]             8-16 bars    ~35s    Bigger arrangement
[Bridge]             4-8 bars     ~20s    Most vulnerable moment
[Final Chorus]       8-16 bars    ~40s    Catharsis, key change, full power
[Outro]              4-8 bars     ~15s    Strip back to intro instrumentation
```

**Total: ~4:10**

**Member distribution guide:**
- Verse 1: Most emotionally open member (Serena or Evie)
- Verse 2: Contrasting voice (Kaia's raw delivery adds weight)
- Verse 3: Third member or return to Verse 1 member with new perspective
- Chorus: Serena lead, layered harmonies from all
- Bridge: The member with the most unexpected emotional range
- Final Chorus: Everyone, full power

## Template 5: Short-Form / TikTok-Optimized (2:30 - 3:00)

```
[Chorus]             8 bars       ~20s    Hook first (the viral moment)
[Verse 1]            8 bars       ~20s    Quick story setup
[Pre-Chorus]         4 bars       ~10s    Fast build
[Chorus]             8 bars       ~20s    Hook repeat
[Verse 2]            8 bars       ~20s    Quick development
[Chorus]             8-16 bars    ~25s    Extended hook, ad-libs
[Bridge]             4 bars       ~10s    Brief emotional shift
[Final Chorus]       8 bars       ~20s    Last hook, decisive ending
```

**Total: ~2:45**

**Member distribution guide:**
- Opening Chorus: Lead vocal hook (must be instantly catchy)
- Verses: One member each, fast and punchy
- Every section earns its place -- nothing filler
- Bridge: Shortest member spotlight, dramatic shift

## Section Energy Mapping

For game integration, each section type maps to gameplay energy:

| Section Type | Energy Level | Game Parallel |
|-------------|-------------|---------------|
| Intro | Low-Mid | Calm / Setup |
| Verse | Mid | Sustain |
| Pre-Chorus | Rising | Buildup |
| Chorus | High | Drop / Peak |
| Post-Chorus | High-Mid | Sustain (elevated) |
| Bridge | Variable (usually low) | Breakdown |
| Dance Break | High | Drop variant |
| Rap Break | High | Sustain (intense) |
| Outro | Falling | Cooldown |
